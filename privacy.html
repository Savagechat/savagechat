Privacy Policy

Savage Chat is designed to be simple, fast, and privacy-minded. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your choices.

Last updated: 12 February 2026

Summary

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We only collect what we need to operate the app and improve reliability.
  • Payments are handled by Apple. We do not store your card details.
  • You can request deletion of certain data by contacting us.

Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to Savage Chat (the “App”). If you have questions, contact us at savagechat@mail.com.

Information we collect

1) Information you provide

  • Chat content you type into the App.
  • Support messages if you email us.

2) Purchases and subscriptions

  • Subscription purchases are processed by Apple via In-App Purchase.
  • We may receive basic purchase/entitlement status (e.g., whether Ultra is active) to unlock features.

3) Usage & diagnostics (optional / minimal)

  • App performance and crash information to improve stability.
  • Basic analytics (e.g., feature usage) if enabled on your device.

Note: Exact data collected can depend on the services you enable (e.g., device analytics settings) and app configuration.

How we use your information

  • To provide the chat experience and deliver responses.
  • To process and verify subscription access (via Apple).
  • To prevent abuse, spam, or fraudulent activity.
  • To troubleshoot issues and improve app reliability.
  • To respond to support requests.

Chat content

Your chat messages are used to generate responses. Depending on how the app is implemented, chat content may be transmitted to a server to process requests.

Avoid sharing sensitive information (e.g., passwords, bank details, medical records) in chats.

Data sharing

We may share limited information with:

  • Apple for payments/subscriptions (In-App Purchases).
  • Service providers that help us run the app (e.g., hosting, diagnostics), only as needed and under appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal compliance if required by law or to protect users and the service.

We do not sell your personal data.

Advertising

If the app shows ads (e.g., rewarded ads), ad providers may collect device identifiers or related signals to serve and measure ads. You can control ad personalization in your device settings where available.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods can vary by data type.

Your choices & rights

  • You can manage your subscription through Apple at Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • You can request help or deletion requests by emailing savagechat@mail.com.
  • You can disable certain analytics in iOS settings (where applicable).

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

Children

Savage Chat is not intended for children under the age required by your local laws. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections are in place.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above.
